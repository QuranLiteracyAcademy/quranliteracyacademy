Courses

Discover the comprehensive Quranic courses that cover reading, learning, memorizing, and understanding the Holy Quran in depth.

Quran Classes for Kids

Many prefer teaching the Quran to children at home. We offer specialized courses for kids to learn the teachings of the Quran effectively.

Quran Classes for Adults

We provide Quran classes tailored for adults, helping them deepen their understanding and connection with the Quran.

Quran Memorization

Memorizing the Holy Quran involves committing it to both mind and heart. It is a divine miracle that Muslims can learn the Quran word by word. At Quran Literacy Academy, we make this process easy and accessible for everyone.

Tajweed

Learn to read the Quran online with Tajweed. Our course teaches proficiency in Quranic recitation by adhering to the rules of Tajweed. Through this course, students can recite the Quran accurately and without errors.

Naurani Qaida

Master the fundamentals of Quranic reading with our Noorani Qaida course. This essential course provides a solid foundation in Arabic letters and pronunciation, preparing students for fluent and correct Quranic recitation.

Tafseer

Deepen your understanding of the Quran by learning Tafseer. We provide detailed explanations and interpretations of Quranic verses, helping students gain insights into the context, meanings, and teachings of the Holy Quran.

Ahadees

Enhance your knowledge of Islamic teachings by learning about the sayings and traditions of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH).

Miscellaneous

Learn and understand the Six Kalimas. Develop a comprehensive understanding of Namaz. Explore the principles and applications of Islamic jurisprudence (Fiqh).
